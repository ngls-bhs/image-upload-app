## Features

- Upload images without login
- Temporary and permanent image hosting
- Temporary image hosting with auto-expiring links for privacy-focused sharing
- Share images instantly via direct links
- Optimized for Discord, Reddit, and online forums

## Use Case

Image Upload is designed for users who need a fast and simple way to upload images and instantly get shareable links, without creating an account or dealing with complex settings.

It is ideal for:
- Sharing images in chats, forums, and social platforms
- Temporary image hosting for privacy-sensitive content
- Quick uploads for developers, designers, and content creators
